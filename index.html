4 PICS 1 WORD

A game where you guess the missing word using four pictures! For instructions, scroll down below.

How To Play The Game:


  1. Press the START button below the title to enter the game
  2. The player must identify the word being described by the pictures presented.
  3. To input a character, letters from the list of available alphabets are to be clicked.(Note that a certain letter can only be used once)
  4. To clear a speific blank, click on the desired letter.
  5. The player's score may increase by 25 points once the submission is correct.
  6. Two(2) hints are given per level to help players in answering the given items
